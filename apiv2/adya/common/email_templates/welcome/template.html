Here are some of the features that Adya provides to manage and protect your apps:
Centralized Administration

You can use the Adya console to add and remove users from Google groups, from Slack channels, allow users to manage their own apps (aka "self-service mode") and take steps when on-boarding or off-boarding users. The goal is to learn only one tool - Adya - to perform the most critical tasks for your cloud applications.
License Management

With Adya you get visibility into your SaaS licenses from a central console to understand the true cost of your SaaS infrastructure. No more running around with spreadsheets! You can see how many licenses are provisioned, how many are actually being used and where you could save money by reclaiming inactive or dormant licenses.
Security and Compliance

Adya provides the following features to help administrators secure data on their SaaS applications:
- See which external users have access to internal documents, which employees are sharing data and control their access (Video)
- Limit exposure to sensitive documents (Video)
- Review and control dangerous third-party applications (Video)
- Set policies and receive alerts to control dangerous apps and data exposure
Auditing and Reporting

With Adya you can get activity log reports by user, group, file or folder. In addition, get access permissions reports on sensitive files or folders. Reports can be on-demand or scheduled reports. Pre-built and custom reports are supported.
